🚀 Project Overview

NexHub is a revolutionary platform that combines Solana's mobile ecosystem with AI capabilities, creating a seamless and intelligent blockchain experience. Our platform introduces the first-ever AI SDK for Solana Mobile that connects agents to multiple Solana protocols, similar to SEND_AI_SDK, while also featuring an AI-controlled launchpad system.
